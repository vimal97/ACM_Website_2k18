About MITS

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) which is located at Puthencruz Ernakulam is promoted by Muthoot M George.It was established with the aim of providing better education to the students as well as to concrete the young minds with the intention to build up our nation.

About ACM

ACM members actively conduct programmes to accomplish the mission and vision of the Computer Science Department and to bring others to our perspective. This association provides additional information to the students through talks and other activities.

ACM-W Initiative at MITS

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women. .The wing is currently under the guidance of Gopika Chandrakumar.For further information Visit here...
